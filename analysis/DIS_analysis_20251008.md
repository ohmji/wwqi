## Executive Summary
**Investment Thesis**: Disney represents a quality business trading at reasonable valuations with strong competitive moats through its IP portfolio and global brand recognition. **Recommendation**: BUY - Strong competitive position with multiple revenue streams, trading below intrinsic value
**Current Price**: $112.53 (Oct 8, 2025) - Down 1.75% week, -5.37% month, +18.34% year
